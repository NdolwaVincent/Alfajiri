About Us

Alfajiri Academy is a premier private institution located in Kenya, dedicated to providing a holistic education that blends academic excellence with practical skills. Our mission is to empower students to become confident, capable individuals ready to face the challenges of the future.

We offer a diverse curriculum that caters to both academic and skill development, ensuring our students have the knowledge and competencies to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Skills and Programs

At Alfajiri Academy, we believe in nurturing individual talents. Our skill-based programs include:

Music

Students learn to play various musical instruments, vocal training, and music theory.

Computer Science

Courses cover coding, digital literacy, and essential technology skills.

Robotics

Hands-on classes that teach engineering concepts and programming.

Karate

Karate classes promote discipline, fitness, and self-defense skills.

Acrobatics

Creative classes that enhance flexibility, balance, and body control.

Shoe Making

Students learn craftsmanship and entrepreneurial skills in shoemaking.

Bakery

Baking classes where students master techniques and food safety practices.

And More...

We continuously expand our curriculum to include new and exciting skills!

Mission

To offer a well-rounded education that blends strong academics with hands-on skills like music, computer science, robotics, shoemaking, and karate. We strive to build students' critical thinking, creativity, and values, equipping them to succeed in a fast-evolving world.

Vision

To be a leading institution that nurtures well-rounded, skillful, and morally grounded students, equipped to excel academically and contribute innovatively to their communities and the world.
